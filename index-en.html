🌿 Pai D’égua Amazon Store

It all started when a group of UEPA students tried to make tacacá in the chemistry lab. The jambu escaped the pot, the tucupi boiled too much, and someone shouted, "This is too 'pai d'égua'!" That's when the idea was born: to create a store with regional kits so no one would have to become a chemist to taste the Amazon.
