🌿 Amazônia Pai D’égua Store

It all started when a group of UEPA students tried to make tacacá in the chemistry lab. The jambu escaped the pot, the tucupi boiled too much, and someone shouted: “This is so ‘pai d’égua’!” That’s when the idea was born: to create a store with regional kits so no one had to become a chemist to taste the Amazon.
